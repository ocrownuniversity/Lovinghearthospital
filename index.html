About Us

A Legacy of Compassionate Healthcare

Founded in 2009, Loving Heart Hospital has been a beacon of health and hope in our community for over 15 years — growing from a small clinic to a 200-bed multi-specialty hospital.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology, staffed by board-certified physicians, driven by one belief — every patient deserves love and respect.

Founded

Built on a Foundation of Love

Loving Heart Hospital was established in 2009 with a single operating theatre and a team of five dedicated professionals. Today, we are a 200-bed multi-specialty hospital.

We have expanded facilities, brought in internationally trained specialists, and adopted the latest technologies — all while maintaining the compassionate care that defines us.

◎

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, compassionate, and technologically advanced healthcare to every individual — treating the whole person with love, dignity, and respect.

◉

Our Vision

To become West Africa's leading healthcare institution — recognized for clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and pioneering medical research across the continent.
