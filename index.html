Our History

Built on a Foundation of Love

Loving Heart Hospital was established in 2009 with a single operating theatre and a team of five dedicated professionals. Today, we are a 200-bed multi-specialty hospital serving thousands annually.

Over the years we have expanded facilities, brought in internationally trained specialists, and adopted the latest technologies — all while maintaining the compassionate care that defines us.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, compassionate, and technologically advanced healthcare to every individual — treating not just the body, but the whole person — with love, dignity, and respect.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's leading healthcare institution — recognized for clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and pioneering medical research that improves lives across the continent.
