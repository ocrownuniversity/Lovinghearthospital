About Us

Our Story of Healing

🏥
2009
Founded

Built on a Foundation of Love

Loving Heart Hospital was established in 2009 at Cashier Agbara, Ogun State. We started as a small clinic and have grown into a trusted multi-specialty hospital serving thousands of patients annually.

We have expanded facilities, brought in internationally trained specialists, and adopted the latest technologies — all while maintaining compassionate care that defines us.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, compassionate, and technologically advanced healthcare to every individual — treating the whole person with love, dignity, and respect.

Our Vision

To become a leading healthcare institution — recognized for clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and pioneering medical research that improves lives in Ogun State and beyond.
