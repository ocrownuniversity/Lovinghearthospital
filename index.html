Our Story

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Loving Heart Hospital was established with a singular vision: to bring world-class, compassionate healthcare to the people of Agbara and its environs in Ogun State, Nigeria.

Founded over a decade ago, we began as a small healthcare clinic serving the immediate Cashier community. Through dedicated service, community trust, and continuous investment in infrastructure and human capital, we have grown into a full-service hospital with modern diagnostic, surgical, and therapeutic facilities.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of patients annually across Agbara, Ota, and the wider Ogun State corridor. Our team works tirelessly to ensure every patient receives not just medical treatment, but genuine care and attention.

At Loving Heart, we believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege. That belief drives every decision we make — from our affordable pricing to our 24/7 emergency services.
