Our Story

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Loving Heart Hospital was established with a singular vision: to bring world-class, compassionate healthcare to the people of Agbara and its environs in Ogun State, Nigeria.

Founded over a decade ago, we began as a small healthcare clinic serving the Agbara community. Through dedicated service, community trust, and continuous investment in infrastructure and human capital, we have grown into a full-service hospital with modern diagnostic, surgical, and therapeutic facilities at Plot 27, Block 3, OPIC Estate Agbara, Ogun State.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of patients annually across Agbara, Ota, and the wider Ogun State corridor. Our team works tirelessly to ensure every patient receives not just medical treatment, but genuine care and attention.

At Loving Heart, we believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege. That belief drives every decision we make — from our affordable pricing to our 24/7 emergency services.

⚡ Quick Actions

📖 How to Manage the Website

🖼️ Hero Banner

Tap Hero / Banner in the sidebar (or ☰ More on mobile). Edit the big title, subtitle, background image and statistics. Tap 💾 Save & Apply — it updates the homepage instantly.

🏥 Services

Tap Services in sidebar. See all services listed. Tap ✏️ Edit to change name/description/image. Tap 🗑 Delete to remove. Tap + Add Service to add a new one. Live on website instantly.

👨‍⚕️ Doctors

Tap Doctors. Tap + Add Doctor to add (name, specialty, photo URL, bio). Tap ✏️ Edit to update. Tap 🗑 Delete to remove. Doctors page updates immediately.

📰 News & Health Tips

Tap News & Tips. Tap + New Post — enter title, category, image URL, summary. Tap ✏️ or 🗑 to edit/delete any post. Posts appear on website instantly.

📅 Appointments

Tap Appointments. Tap View for full details, ✅ Confirm to confirm, or call/WhatsApp the patient directly. Tap 🗑 Delete to remove a booking.

💊 HMO Plans

Tap HMO Plans. Add or delete accepted health insurance plans. They show on the HMO section of the homepage.

⚙️ Settings

Tap Settings to update phone, email, address, opening hours and HMS link. Tap 💾 Save & Apply — contact info updates everywhere on the website immediately.

📱 Using on Mobile Phone

Use the bottom bar for quick access to Home, Appointments, Doctors, News. Tap ☰ More to open the full menu for Hero, About, Services, HMO, Messages and Settings.
